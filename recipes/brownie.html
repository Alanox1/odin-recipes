Brownie

A brownie, a delectable dessert marvel, entices with its rich, fudgy interior and a delicate crackly top. With each bite, the perfect balance of chocolatey indulgence and irresistible chewiness unfolds, creating a moment of pure dessert bliss.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Walnuts (optional)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the desired temperature.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan and stir in the cocoa powder.
  3. Whisk in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Gently fold in the flour and salt until just incorporated.
  5. If desired, mix in chopped walnuts or other add-ins.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan and spread evenly.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for the specified time, or until a toothpick comes out with a few moistcrumbs.
  8. Let the brownies cool in the pan before cutting into squares.
  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade brownies!
